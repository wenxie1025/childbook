Scene 1

Far away in the sky lived a star named Little Ming. Little Ming always felt she wasn’t shiny enough.

Scene 2

“Can I do something special too?” Little Ming whispered to the wind. But the wind didn’t reply.

Scene 3

Little Ming decided to set off on a journey to Earth, where human children lived, in search of an answer.

Scene 4

She flew down and lit up the face of a little bear. “What’s wrong?” she asked gently.

Scene 5

“I can’t find my way home. Thank you for helping me,” said the little bear. Little Ming felt a warm glow in her heart.

Scene 6

Little Ming discovered that her light could bring warmth and happiness.

Scene 7

“Maybe… it’s time to go back,” Little Ming said to her new friends.

Scene 8

Her light shone brighter than ever. Everyone could see it.

Scene 9

“I shine to light the way for others,” Little Ming finally understood.

Scene 10

From that day on, Little Ming stayed in the night sky, watching over the dreams of every child.
